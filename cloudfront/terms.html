Program Name

Neuralpreneur AI — Firm OS Client Intake SMS Program

Program Description

Neuralpreneur AI provides an AI-powered SMS client intake service to US law firms. When you text a law firm's designated number, you will receive automated SMS messages from our intake assistant to collect basic information about your legal inquiry and connect you with the appropriate attorney.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You will typically receive between 2–10 messages per intake conversation. After your intake is complete, messages are limited to case-related updates from the law firm.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your plan.

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. You will receive one confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.
