Neuralpreneur AI Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 22, 2026 | Last Updated: April 24, 2026

Neuralpreneur AI ("we," "our," or "us") provides AI-assisted client intake and case communication services to law firms through our Firm OS platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with us via SMS.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your mobile phone number
  • The content of SMS messages you send to our system
  • Your preferred language
  • Information you voluntarily provide during intake (name, case details)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your legal inquiry and facilitate communication with the law firm
  • To route urgent matters to the appropriate attorney
  • To maintain a record of your intake and case communication

3. We Do Not

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Use your information for marketing unrelated to your legal matter
  • Share your information with any party other than the law firm you contacted and authorized service providers

4. SMS Messaging

By texting our number, you consent to receive SMS responses related to your legal inquiry. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for assistance.

5. Data Security

We store all client data in encrypted, access-controlled databases. We follow industry best practices to protect your information from unauthorized access.

6. Data Retention

Intake and conversation records are retained as required by applicable law and the law firm's record-keeping obligations.
